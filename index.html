about us

Welcome to Kanine Klans! Set against a polluted Earth, our game begins with four visionary scientists determined to explore humanity’s potential beyond terrestrial boundaries. They send forth four intrepid Kanines, transformed into humanoid forms, to experience and conquer the Martian environment.

In Kanine Klans, you command powerful Rovers and Humanoid Kanines—each with unique elemental powers of Fire, Water, Air, and Land. Your mission is to conquer territories, craft a thriving ecosystem on Mars, and master these elemental powers. Navigate through diverse landscapes, strategize battles, and rise to supremacy amidst emerging rivalries.
